USER CURATED

Codon relies on the consensus of its users when determining the most useful and interesting projects. Users can give "tubes" to projects showcasing important and groundbreaking research initiatives.

CROWDFUNDED

Scientists are able to supplement their research through crowdfunded donations. Contributors could be considered a recognized donor on any papers published (per the project owner's discretion).

VERSION CONTROLLED

Coming soon: Codon will be version controlled so that all edits to projects and articles made on the platform will be preserved. This makes it easy to fix mistakes and view past work. Think of it like Github for scientific papers.
